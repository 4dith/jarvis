ABOUT US
The AI and Robotics Club is a dynamic community uniting students passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. Our goal is to spark curiosity, drive innovation, and apply emerging technologies for real-world impact.

Members from diverse fields—Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, and Mechanical—collaborate on interdisciplinary projects, blending skills to create and innovate together.

Mission:
To foster innovation and collaboration through hands-on projects, empowering members to solve real-world problems and benefit society.

ACTIVITIES
Workshops, guest lectures, and discussions on AI and robotics
Project building and brainstorming sessions
Opportunities for members to propose and join projects

RESOURCES
Access to hardware such as microcontrollers, sensors, actuators, and other electronics, along with tutorials and practical workshops.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What activities does the club offer?
Workshops, project sessions, lectures, and discussions on AI and robotics.

Do I need prior experience?
No. We welcome all skill levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

How can I join projects?
Attend brainstorming sessions, join ongoing projects, or propose new ideas. Team collaboration is encouraged.

What learning resources are provided?
Members gain access to hardware tools, tutorials, and guided hands-on learning.
